The Safe360 Story

Bridging the Gap Between Compliance & Reality

Safe360 is a student-led enterprise project (CET257) built to address the fatal flaws in traditional safety management. Most workplace accidents are preventable through better data and faster communication. Our platform digitizes these workflows to save time—and more importantly—save lives.

Our Vision

To create zero-harm workspaces globally through real-time hazard intelligence and predictive data modeling.

Our Mission

To deliver an accessible, enterprise-grade safety platform that empowers every worker on the floor.
